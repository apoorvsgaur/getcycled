What is GetCycled?

Tired of bringing your bottles into the liquor store to get them returned and refunded? Our GetCycled bottle machines address this issue by allowing you to return your bottles in exchange for a cash balance on your account. Then at your convenience, you can go cash out your balance at the liquor store!
